Spinach, Onion, and Goat Cheese Omelettes

By Laseandra Kelly

I make spinach, onion, and goat cheese omelettes whenever I am feeling fancy and have goat cheese in my fridge. The spinach makes it healthy and the goat cheese makes me feel fancy and tastes really good.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 medium Onion
    		•
  • 2 tbsp. Butter
    		•
  • 1 large Handful of Spinach
    		•
  • 3 large Eggs
    		•
  • 2 tbsp. Heavy Cream
    		•
  • 1 oz. Goat Cheese
    		•
  • 1 medium Spring Onion(Garnish)
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to Taste
    		•

    DIRECTIONS

  • Melt butter in pan
  • Cut 1/4 onion into long strips while butter browns
  • add onion to browning butter and let caramelize
  • Once onions are translucent, add spinach to pan and let cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper
  • Remove onion and spinach mixture and set aside. It small container crack 3 large eggs
  • add heavy cream, salt, and pepper and mix together
  • Heat pan to medium-low heat, add egg mixture and allow to cook
  • Once edges begin to set, and onion and spinach mixture onto half the omelette and crumble goat cheese on top and season with more salt and pepper
  • Fold omelette is half and garnish with chopped spring onions
  • Enjoy!
