Spinach, Onion, and Goat Cheese Omelettes

By Laseandra Kelly

I make spinach, onion, and goat cheese omelettes whenever I am feeling fancy and have goat cheese in my fridge. The spinach makes it healthy and the goat cheese makes me feel fancy and tastes really good.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 medium Onion
  • 2 tbsp. Butter
  • 1 large Handful of Spinach
  • 3 large Eggs
  • 2 tbsp. Heavy Cream
  • 1 oz. Goat Cheese
  • 1 medium Spring Onion(Garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to Taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Melt butter in pan
  2. Cut 1/4 onion into long strips while butter browns
  3. add onion to browning butter and let caramelize
  4. Once onions are translucent, add spinach to pan and let cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Remove onion and spinach mixture and set aside. It small container crack 3 large eggs
  6. add heavy cream, salt, and pepper and mix together
  7. Heat pan to medium-low heat, add egg mixture and allow to cook
  8. Once edges begin to set, and onion and spinach mixture onto half the omelette and crumble goat cheese on top and season with more salt and pepper
  9. Fold omelette is half and garnish with chopped spring onions
  10. Enjoy!
